Objective

Be the first player to reach 100 points by rolling a dice and deciding when to hold.



How to Play


  1. Roll the Die:
    • If you roll 2-6, the number is added to your round score.
    • If you roll 1, you lose your round score and your turn ends.

  2. Choose an Action:
    • Roll Again Keep rolling to increase your round score.
    • Hold Add your round score to your total score and end your turn.

  3. Winning the Game: The first player to reach or exceed 100 points wins!
